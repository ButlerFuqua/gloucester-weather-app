Privacy Policy

App: Gloucester Weather App
Last updated: April 20, 2026

Summary

The Gloucester Weather App does not collect, store, or share any personal information about its users. The app displays weather data for Gloucester, Virginia using a fixed set of coordinates.

Information the App Does Not Collect

  • The app does not request access to your device's location.
  • The app does not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • The app does not use advertising, analytics, or tracking services.
  • The app does not create user accounts or store any data on a server.

Third-Party Services

To display current weather, the app makes requests to the OpenWeatherMap API. These requests contain only the name of a fixed location (Gloucester, Virginia) and an API key. No information about you, your device, or your location is sent. You can review OpenWeatherMap's own privacy practices at openweather.co.uk/privacy-policy.

Children's Privacy

The app is safe for all ages. It does not knowingly collect any information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
